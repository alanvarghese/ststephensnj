Sunday School

"Train a child in the way he should go, and when he is old, he won't depart from it."
Proverbs: 22.6
Sunday school Timing: 9.00 am to 10.15 am

With the grace of God Almighty, St.Stephen's Orthodox Church has a fully fledged Sunday school consisting of 52 students and 18 teachers attending classes from Kindergarten to 12th grade. Following are the teachers: Abey.K.Tharian, Leena Varghese, Aju Tharian, Aleyamma John, Jimmy John, Leena George, Valsamma Joseph, Elizabeth Mathew, Shobhana Joseph, Lincy Thomas, Saly Abraham, Anu Abraham, Sunitha Varghese, Nizy Roy, Joshila Mathew, Aleyamma Varghese, Bindu John and Titus Mathew.

Five students from grade 5, three students from grade 8, and four students from grade 10 and 2 students from 12th grade passed the Centralized Sunday School Examination of the North East American Diocese. Angelle Philip and Jonathan Kurian shared the third rank and Stephen John scored fourth rank in the centralized examination. Timothy Mathew and Nizy Roy made history by appearing in the 12th grade Centralized Examination for the first time from our church. Timothy won the third rank in the Diocese. Also Aju Tharian passed the Teacher Training Certificate (TTC) examination. To encourage our Sunday school children, we have introduced cash awards from our church sponsors for the highest scoring children in fifth, eighth and tenth grade centralized examinations. These children should score 80 or more to be eligible for this award. This year, Mrs.Ponnama Mammen Memorial $ 50 each cash awards sponsored by Johnson Thomas and family were given to Angelle Philip and Jonathan Kurian from the 5th grade, Mr.O.M.Kunjukunju $100 cash award sponsored by Titus Mathew and family was given to George Varghese from 8th grade and Mr and Mrs.T. K. Mathew Memorial $100 cash award sponsored by Mr. Abey K. Tharian and family was given to Rinsu George from the 10th grade.

Our Sunday school children displayed a commendable performance in the Area competitions held at St. Stephen's Orthodox Church, Bergenfield, NJ. We captured five first prizes and seven second prizes in the Area competitions. Congratulations to Angelle Philip for winning the 1st place in the Solo song competition during the North East American Diocese Sunday School Regional Competitions held at St. Thomas Orthodox Church, Philadelphia.

OVBS-2012 was held on July 26 to 28 in our church with the help and co-operation of Sunday school teachers, Marthamariam Samajam members, Church Office bearers and many well wishers. Vicar Rev. Fr. Babu. K.Mathew gave a message based on the theme "We are one in Christ." (Galatians 3: 28). Christmas Eve Program was held with great joy and celebration with the help and participation of all Sunday school children, teachers and parents on Dec 24th 2012. Children and adults performed dances, dramas, instrumental music and sang many carol songs during the event. Sunday school children also visited the Bergen Regional Medical Center and sang Xmas Carol songs and presented handmade gifts for the patients in the long term medical care unit.

Sincere thanks to all members of our church for their continued prayers, support and encouragement of our Sunday school. Also on behalf of the Sunday school teachers and students, I express my deepest gratitude towards our Vicar, Rev. Fr. Babu. K. Mathew for his consistent care, guidance and prayers that makes our Sunday school, the foundation of Orthodox faith.

Mr.Abey.K. Tharian Sunday School Principal

Orthodox Syrian Sunday School Association of the East Northeast American Diocese
